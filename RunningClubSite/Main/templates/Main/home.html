The 2019-2020 season begins!

The new school year is beginning in August and club will be back and ready for another semester of camraderie, mileage, and PR's! After a 2017-18, which saw not only our first NIRCA individual champion, but our second, the club looks to be even better this year. We welcome anyone interested in running, whether it be at a competitive level or just a recreational level to stay in shape! Last year we had record levels of attendance and can't wait to see everyone's smiling and sweaty faces! Be sure to come check us out any weekday at 5:15PM on the east side of the track! This site contains information on meets, practices, and who to contact if you have any questions. We look forward to this season and hope to see you out there!

How do I join?

To join the running club, fill out this survey with all necessary information, pay your dues to @UCRunningClub on venmo (only if you choose to be a NIRCA member). You will need to fill out the Risk Management Forms on CampusLink. The CampusLink forms need to be done in the first week of school, if your first day of club isn't until after the first week, then these need to be done before you can run with us.

If you are over 18, please also fill out the Driver Registration Forms. If you are over 21, please complete the defensive driving course and e-mail your certificate to ucclubrunning@gmail.com.

Once you've done all this, please join the official UC Running Club slack channel! (Slack is a free app that is similar to GroupMe but on steroids, you should download it.) We communicate most everything important through Slack!

Any questions? Shoot Grant, Gunnar, Bryan, Lauren, or Sean an e-mail, text, groupme message, carrier pigeon, or telegraph.
